Wellness is deeply rooted in CozyStay’s DNA.

Complete with treatment rooms, two saunas, and an indoor pool, the spa offers a range of treatments, from traditional massage and facials to concentrated plant power shots and soothing magnesium treatments. Expert therapists draw on a modern and ancient healing techniques to rejuvenate and pamper you in privacy.

Beyond its signature therapies, the spa features dedicated relaxation lounges where guests can unwind before or after treatments. Soft lighting, calming aromatherapy, and gentle ambient music create an atmosphere designed to soothe the senses from the moment you step inside. Whether you prefer quiet solitude or a peaceful shared space, the spa provides the perfect environment to disconnect from daily stress and fully embrace tranquility.
